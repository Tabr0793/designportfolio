UX PROCESS
USER RESEARCH | IA | wireframe | prototypes

I start a project by defining the scope of work and the problem at hand. Then I discover information I need from stakeholders. After conducting user research, interviews and surveys, I begin to devise strategic solutions in the form of sitemaps, wireframes and prototypes. I test the usability of my design and continue to refine my iterations to ensure a great user experience.

UI DESIGN
ui elements | interactions | BRANDING

I create brand language and define unique UI elements for each product. I make creative design decisions to best translate any given UX wireframe into simple, intuitive interactions. I always communicate my design through interactive prototypes and prepare the necessary files for the development team.
