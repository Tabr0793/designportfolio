HI,I'M TAYLOR.

I am a UX-UI designer in Denver, CO passionate about designing digital experiences that leave a positive impact on people, environment and society.

Before I found my way to UX-UI design, I completed my B, ENVD. from the University of Colorado and landed my first job as an architectural designer where I found myself glued to a drafting screen & nose deep in a building code binder when, what I longed to do was connect with people; learn about their needs and help them achieve their goals using my passion for intuitive design. While living in Silicon Valley, I found myself at the crux of a career shift. Surrounded by software engineers & web developers, I was introduced to UX design and decided I’d join their ranks by completing my credential in UX/UI Design from BLOC; a program known for its user-centered approach. Since then, I've adopted a full time career developing my skills in UX and visual design.

To me, design is a problem solving activity. As a designer, I understand that the perfect design not only involves visual aesthetics, but an interface that functions well. This requires a deep understanding of human behavior and psychology - the outcome of which greatly impacts the overall experience of the product, regardless of the platform.

I enjoy working on projects that require both strategy as well as visual design. I love working on problems from the ideation stage and seeing them through completion with pixel-perfect designs. My goal is to become a domain expert in my field; able to move among the process from user research to designing then shipping and analyzing results.
