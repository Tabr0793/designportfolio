HI,I'M TAYLOR.
—a UX-UI designer in Denver, CO

My most recent work stint was with Frontier Airlines — one of the largest ultra low-cost carrier airlines in the US. The objective was to reimagine the way the airline interacts with their customers from the moment they decide to purchase a plane ticket to the moment they land.

Having designed the new website and leading the design team building Frontier’s new mobile app, taught me how to balance business goals and engineering constraints as I unrelentingly advocated for the user.

I graduated with a B. ENVD, Architecture from the University of Colorado and completed my credential in UX/UI Design from BLOC; a program known for its user-center approach to design. Ever since, I've been designing with great care and empathy towards users.
